# OPCC Code of Conduct

OPCC welcomes constructive participation from contributors, reviewers, and
users. Be respectful, assume good faith, focus criticism on ideas and evidence,
and protect privacy and licence obligations.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, discrimination, threats, personal
attacks, sharing private or restricted data, or knowingly misrepresenting data
provenance or licensing.

Report conduct concerns privately to the current project maintainers through
the contact method in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md). Maintainers will acknowledge
the report, protect confidentiality as practical, assess it fairly, and may
warn, restrict participation, remove content, or bar participation. A
maintainer with a conflict of interest does not decide that report.
